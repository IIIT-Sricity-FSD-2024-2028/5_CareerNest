## Design Overview

The Figma prototype contains the complete UI design for the **CareerNest Placement Management System**.  
It includes interfaces for different user roles such as:

- Candidate
- Recruiter
- Alumni
- Placement Officer

The design demonstrates the workflow of the placement process including opportunity browsing, application management, referrals, and recruitment tracking.
